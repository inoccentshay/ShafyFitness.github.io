Shafy Fitness Rules and Policies.


Memberships:

  • Membership Options: We offer various membership tiers to suit your needs. Please visit our website or consult a staff member for details
  • Guest Policy: Guests are welcome with a paid daily pass or accompanied by a valid Shafy Fitness member.
  • Membership Fees: Membership fees are automatically charged monthly (or according to your chosen billing cycle) on your registered payment method. Please refer to your membership agreement for details.
  • Membership Cancellation: Memberships can be cancelled with a 30-day written notice (email or in-person)

Gym Etiquette:

  • Facility Hours: Please adhere to the posted gym hours.
  • Dress Code: Wear appropriate athletic attire and closed-toe shoes for safety reasons. Jeans, street clothes, and open-toed footwear are not permitted in workout areas.
  • Equipment Usage: Wipe down equipment before and after use with provided sanitizing wipes. Return weights and equipment to their designated locations after use.
  • Sharing Equipment: When multiple people are waiting for the same equipment, limit your workout time to a reasonable duration (consider circuit training or asking staff for guidance).
  • Courtesy: Be mindful of others. Avoid excessive noise or grunting. Refrain from using your phone for calls while using equipment.
  • Food & Drinks: Only bring water bottles with secure lids into workout areas. Food consumption is restricted to designated areas.

Safety

  • Lockers: We recommend using lockers to secure your belongings. Shafy Fitness is not responsible for lost or stolen items.
  • Free Weights: If you're new to weightlifting, it's advisable to seek guidance from a trainer to ensure proper form and avoid injury.
  • Spotting: When using heavy weights, always have a spotter present for safety.
  • Report Injuries: Inform staff immediately of any injuries sustained while using the gym facilities
  • Medical Conditions: If you have any pre-existing medical conditions, consult your doctor before starting a new exercise program.

Guest Policy:

  • Guests must be 18 years or older or accompanied by a valid Shafy Fitness member (age restrictions may apply).
  • Guests are required to sign a waiver acknowledging the gym rules and releasing Shafy Fitness from liability.
  • Daily guest passes can be purchased at the front desk.

General

  • Smoking: Smoking is strictly prohibited inside the gym and on the premises.
  • Solicitation: Soliciting or promoting personal businesses or services within the gym is not permitted.
  • Abusive Behavior: Any behavior deemed disruptive, threatening, or abusive towards staff or members will not be tolerated and may result in membership termination.
We appreciate your cooperation in maintaining a positive and respectful environment for everyone at Shafy Fitness. If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to ask a staff member.
