Shafy Fitness Class Schedule

Staying active and reaching your fitness goals is easier with a consistent workout routine. Shafy Fitness offers a variety of group fitness classes designed to cater to all interests and fitness levels. Here's a glimpse into our schedule:

Class Types

  • Cardio: Get your heart pumping and burn calories with high-energy classes like Zumba, Spin, and HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training).
  • Strength Training: Build muscle and improve overall strength with classes focused on weightlifting, bodyweight exercises, and resistance training.
  • Mind & Body: Enhance your flexibility, reduce stress, and improve core strength with Yoga, Pilates, and Barre classes.

Benefits of Group Fitness Classes:

There are many benefits to participating in group fitness classes. Here are a few:

  • Motivation & Support: Working out with others can help you stay motivated and accountable.
  • Variety & Fun: Group fitness classes offer a variety of workouts to keep things interesting and prevent plateaus.
  • Expert Guidance: Our certified instructors provide guidance and ensure you perform exercises safely and effectively.
  • Community: Connect with like-minded individuals and build a supportive fitness community.
